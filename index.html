Tests

These are some common tests that determine the cause and nature of diseases by examining and testing body tissues (from biopsies and pap smears, for example) and bodily fluids (from samples including blood and urine). The results from these pathology tests help doctors diagnose and treat patients correctly. Here’s a list of the health tests that everyone should undertake as part of a healthy lifestyle.

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Price: ₹450

It is used to diagnose anaemia, infections, certain types of cancer, and so on. It’s especially important for Indian women as they tend to suffer from iron deficiency and anaemia and may require supplementation. If the CBC is okay, it may be repeated once a year.

Blood Sugar Test

Price: ₹360

Done after a 12-hour fasting period, this helps detect diabetes. In cases of pre-diabetes and diabetes, an additional test, HbA1C that indicates the average blood sugar levels over the previous 3 months is done. An annual testing is recommended if the reading is normal.

Lipid Profile

Price: ₹990

It is an accurate indicator of your heart health. This blood test measures the total cholesterol, triglycerides, and HDL and LDL levels. Testing once in 2 years is recommended for people with normal readings.

Liver Function Test

Price: ₹1070

This is done annually to screen for liver conditions, such as alcohol-induced liver damage, fatty liver, Hepatitis C and B.

Urine Analysis

Price: ₹780

It checks for presence of proteins, sugar and blood (especially in smokers who are at high risk for bladder cancer) in the urine sample, which could indicate kidney disease, among other conditions. If the report is normal, get a test done yearly.

Thyroid Function Test

Price: ₹570

These blood tests are important in detecting underactive (hypothyroidism) or overactive thyroid (hyperthyroidism). In case of normal findings, once-a-year testing is recommended.

Endocrinology

Endocrinologists treat people who suffer from hormonal imbalances, typically from glands in the endocrine system or certain types of cancers. The overall goal of treatment is to restore the normal balance of hormones found in a patient's body.

The Endocrinology Laboratory supports Medilabs and is committed to providing the highest level of diagnostic specimen testing for people with endocrine system disorders. We adhere to international standards and best practices.
